Hello friends!

I’m an undergraduate pursuing a major in Computer Science and a minor in Technology Management at UC Davis. This summer, I was the Application Development Intern at the State Bar where I helped build an online management system that automates the processing and automating of records.

I enjoy using programming as a tool to express my creativity, act on my ideas, and achieve my vision. I was born and raised in San Francisco, which helped me naturally develop an affinity for technology; however, my passion for coding truly flourished when I became one of the frontend web developers at Citrus Hack, UC Riverside’s hackathon organization, last summer.

One year ago, I had virtually no experience with programming. Flash forward to the present, I’m now a passionate full-stack web developer and an upcoming software engineer. As an enthusiastic newbie in tech, I’m always very open with exploring different realms in computer science and learning new technologies.

When I’m not working, I’m probably watching political satire shows, perusing through memes, jamming to lo-fi/indie/R&B music, drinking jasmine green milk tea boba, or contemplating creative means to be more extra than the day before.

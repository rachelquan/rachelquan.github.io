Citrus Hack 2019

01. Background

Citrus Hack is UC Riverside's annual hackathon that attracts hundreds of talented students from all over Southern California to build something fascinating using technology. This year, Citrus Hack has four tracks for participants to select from and create their projects around; these four tracks are the following: Sentinel, Reclaimer, Campaigner, and Medic.

02. Role

I’ve been a part of the marketing team at Citrus Hack as one of the graphic designers since June 2018 and previously worked with the team on creating graphics for Cutie Hack, our beginner hackathon. For this year’s Citrus Hack, I designed a graphic for the Campaigner, one of the hackathon’s four tracks, using Adobe Illustrator. While working on this project, I often collaborated with other designers who are working on graphics for the other tracks to establish a cohesive brand design.

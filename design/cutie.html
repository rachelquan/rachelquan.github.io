Cutie Hack 2018

01. Background

Cutie Hack is UC Riverside’s annual beginner, 12-hour hackathon. The purpose of Cutie Hack is to help make hackathons less intimidating and more approachable for those who have little to no experience with hacking.

02. Role

Since June 2018, I’ve been a part of the marketing team at Citrus Hack as one of the graphic designers. I helped with establishing a consistent and distinctive brand identity for the hackathon and designed social media graphics using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. Please check out my designs below! The logo was created by a fellow graphic designer on the team.
